Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a personal pudú?

Please don't, they are classified as endangered species.

Where can I see a pudú?

Southern Chile would be the best choice.

What does pudú eat?

Vines, leaves from low trees, shrubs, succulent sprouts, herbs, ferns, blossoms, buds, tree bark, and fallen fruit.

Do female pudús have antlers?

Unfortunately, no.
