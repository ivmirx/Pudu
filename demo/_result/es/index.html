Pudú

This is our deer.

Pudú dramatically improves the most important aspects of deer experience.
The smallest size and roundest shape ever in a deer.
Short, spiked antlers that are not forked.
And it looks every bit as cute as it is.
This is Pudú – the world's smallest deer.

Design

Pudú takes an incredibly popular deer design and refines it even further: the fur is long and stiff with a reddish-brown to dark-brown hue.

Dimensions

Deers have never been that light. Just 6.4 to 13.4 kg (14 to 30 lb) in weight and 35 to 45 cm (14 to 18 in) tall at the shoulder.

Habitat

Living in gorgeous temperate rainforests of South America, Pudú hides from predators in dense underbrush and bamboo thickets.
